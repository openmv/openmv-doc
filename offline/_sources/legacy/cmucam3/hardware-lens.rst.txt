CMUcam Lens
===========

The CMUcam3 usually comes with the C3088 ov6620 camera module. Be careful, there
could be changes in the lens mount depending on available parts.

* Camera with lens f4.9mm, F2.8.
* IR cut filter
* Weight: 9 grams (0.32 oz)
