Sample Images
=============

This page has various sample images that can be used for various purposes.

The ``conference.png`` and ``kitchen.png`` sample images were not preserved in the
archive.

**PPM Virtual-Cam Samples**

* Virtual-Cam Samples 1 (``vcam-imgs.zip``, not preserved in the archive)

  * Zip archive with 18 random images that can be used with the
    :doc:`virtual-cam <virtual-cam>`

**JPEG Samples**

* Samples 1 (``samples-1.zip``, not preserved in the archive)

  * Zip archive with 163 images from various different environments

* Samples 2 (``samples-2.zip``, not preserved in the archive)

  * Zip archive with 200 images from an office environment

* Samples 3 (``samples-2.zip``, not preserved in the archive)

  * Zip archive with 157 images from indoor and outside
  * Some images show how quality degrades when the battery runs low (e.g.
    IMG00101.JPG to IMG00113.JPG)
