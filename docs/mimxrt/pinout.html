Legend:

  • Qm/n: QTMR module m, channel n

  • Fm/n/l: FLEXPWM module m, submodule n, channel l. The pulse at a X channel is always aligned to the period start.

Pins without a PWM signal are not listed. A signal may be available at more than one Pin. FlexPWM pins may also be pure CPU pin, not assigned to a board signal. In that case the PWM output is disabled. The PWM channel of a submodule 0 may still be used as synchronization source for other channels of the same module, unless used by another peripheral.
