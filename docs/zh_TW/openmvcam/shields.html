OpenMV 擴充板¶

可插接到 OpenMV Cam 上的擴充板，為其加上網路連線、馬達控制、顯示器、感測器等更多功能。每一頁都會介紹該擴充板的功能、主要規格，以及從 MicroPython 驅動它的 API。

現代擴充板¶

Gigabit PoE Shield

Gigabit Ethernet with PoE for higher-bandwidth streaming.

Explore →

Servo Shield

Drive up to 4 servos drawing up to 5A while powering the camera, 6–36V input.

Explore →

Battery Shield

1.8–5.5V battery input via a DC barrel jack, plus a 6–36V wide input.

Explore →

Touch LCD Shield

2.3" 320×240 SPI LCD with capacitive multi-touch and Qwiic.

Explore →

PoE Shield

10/100 Ethernet with Power-over-Ethernet.

Explore →

PIR Shield

6µA standby motion trigger plus white and 850 nm IR illumination.

Explore →

CAN/RS232 Shield

8 Mb/s CAN-FD plus 1 Mb/s RS-232 for vehicles and legacy serial.

Explore →

RS422/RS485 Shield

10 Mb/s differential serial for long-distance industrial buses.

Explore →

Driver Shield

Dual 3A motor drivers or quad 1.5A line drivers, 6–36V.

Explore →

Relay Shield

Dual 60 W relays for AC/DC load switching, 6–36V input.

Explore →

AE3 配件¶

AE3 OLED Breakout

128×128 RGB OLED, 5‑way joystick, Qwiic, and a 10‑pin ARM SWD header.

AE3 Battery Shield

WiFi Shield

Proto Shield

Pan and Tilt Shield

TV Shield

QWIIC Shield

Light Shield

Nine high-power white LEDs with a TPS61169 driver and DAC/PWM dimming.

CAN Shield
