Lua API: 상수#

A list of the CMUcam3 constants exposed in the Camscripter Lua environment.

Camera Resolution Constants#

  • CC3_CAMERA_RESOLUTION_LOW – low resolution

  • CC3_CAMERA_RESOLUTION_HIGH – high resolution

Channel of Interest (COI) Constants#

  • CC3_CHANNEL_SINGLE – one channel, regardless of image type

  • CC3_CHANNEL_RED – red channel in RGB images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_GREEN – green channel in RGB images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_BLUE – blue channel in RGB images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_Y – Y channel in YCrCb images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_CR – Cr channel in YCrCb images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_CB – Cb channel in YCrCb images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_HUE – Hue channel in HSV images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_SAT – Saturation channel in HSV images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_VAL – Value channel in HSV images

  • CC3_CHANNEL_ALL – All channels

Colorspace Constants#

  • CC3_COLORSPACE_RGB – The RGB colorspace (red, green, blue) http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RGB

  • CC3_COLORSPACE_YCRCB – The YCrCb colorspace. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YCbCr
