Note for European Union Residents
---------------------------------

The WEEE symbol on the CMUcam4 indicates that the CMUcam4 must not be disposed
of with other household waste. Instead, it is your responsibility to dispose of
the CMUcam4 by handing it over to the designated collection point for recycling
of waste electrical equipment. Separate collection and recycling of the CMUcam4
at the time of disposal will help to conserve natural resources and ensure that
the CMUcam4 is recycled in a manner that protects human health and the
environment. For more information about where you can drop off the CMUcam4 for
recycling, please contact your local city office or your local waste disposal
service.
