Analog Video Output
===================

A right mounted SMD header can be attached to the CMUcam3 to provide analog
output to a TV or wireless transmitter. `Digikey <http://www.digikey.com>`__ part
S1113E-36-ND fits the pads on the CMUcam3. Using the OV6620 camera module, you
will be able to get a PAL video signal from the analog port of the CMUcam3. This
will sync up with any PAL monitor, but will not work with a standard NTSC
monitor. The OV7620 camera module will output a standard black and white NTSC
video signal. To use this output, it is necessary to keep the camera at its
maximum frame rate (the default) and switch it into YCrCb mode in order to see
the image on a monitor.
