Camera Bus
==========

This bus interfaces with the CMOS camera chip. The CMOS camera board is mounted
parallel to the processing part of the board and connects starting at pin 1. The
female camera header should be soldered on the back of the main CMUcam3 board.
The CMUcam3 works with the OV6620 and OV7630 camera modules.
