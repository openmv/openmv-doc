Lua API: Constants
==================

This wiki page listed the CamScripter Lua API constants -- including the camera
resolution constants, the colorspace constants (``CC3_COLORSPACE_YCRCB`` and
``CC3_COLORSPACE_RGB``) and the channel of interest constants -- but its content
was not preserved in the archive.

See :doc:`luaapifunctions` for the functions that take these constants as
arguments.
